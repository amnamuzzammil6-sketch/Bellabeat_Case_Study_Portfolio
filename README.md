## 1. The Business Problem
Bellabeat, a high-tech manufacturer of health-focused products for women, wants to analyze smart device usage data to gain insights into how consumers use non-Bellabeat smart devices. The goal is to unlock new growth opportunities for the company.

### Key Questions
1. What are some trends in smart device usage?
2. How could these trends apply to Bellabeat customers?
3. How could these trends help influence Bellabeat marketing strategy?

## 3. Key Insights & Recommendations
Analysis of FitBit Fitness Tracker Data revealed:

* **The "Weekend Drop-Off":** Users are most active on **Wednesdays** and least active on **Sundays**, indicating a need for weekend engagement strategies.
* **Sedentary Behavior:** The average user spends **16.5 hours** sedentary per day.
* **Correlation:** A strong positive correlation (r=0.5) exists between steps and calories, validating "Step Count" as a key marketing metric.

**Strategic Recommendation:** Launch a "Weekend Warrior" campaign and "Sedentary Alerts" feature to drive engagement during low-activity periods.
